Stefanie's Caterpillars Family

Stefanie is a pretty butterfly and mother of a big family. She has eight young caterpillars and they all have special strengths. Here is the photograph of the brothers and sisters.

Sequence Name Strength
1 Lucy Lucy is a genius at music. She can play the piano very well with the help of leaves. When she play the music, the notes come out well and true.
2 Margery Margery is awfully strong. She can lift twelve leaves at the same time. She even won a gold medal on that.
3 Mirabel Mirabel is a math genius. She can solve all kinds of math problems, even the math olympaid ones. I'm sure the gene is from her mother Stefanie since she's a math maniac too.
4 Janet Janet is a big eater. You can have her do all the amazing things with a leaf. For example, if you take a leaf and move it in a circle in the air, she will do a back flip to chase it. When she lands, she will snatch the leaf and gobble it up.
5 Hilary Hilary is thin and long. Her legs are long too hence she can run really fast. Well, at least fast enough as a caterpillar.
6 Gladys Gladys can write very well, even in the murderous Chinese. She's now writting a book and hopefully she will be able to publish it before she turns into a butterfly.
7 Harry Harry is extremely brave and able to recognize danger at the first second. He protects all his elder sisters (6 of them) and younger brother from danger.
8 Albus Albus is the youngest. He is the other boy in the family in addition to Harry. He has fabulous imagination. He's very interested in astronomy and wonders what is beyond the Milky Way. He has even come up a lot of crazy ideas such as talking alians.
